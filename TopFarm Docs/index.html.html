TopFarm Farmer Producer Company Pvt. Ltd.

A company made by farmers for the farmers, collaborating with 200+ farmers across 15+ villages based in Maharashtra.

Specializing in high-quality mushroom cultivation and other essential crops.

Company Overview

Established in 2023 in Tapargaon near Ellora by farmer Mr. Vinod Motinge, with a goal to develop people, processes, and practices that empower smallholder Indian farmers to compete at a global level by producing top-quality fresh Mushrooms and other crops.

Our Farms

Located in 2-3 main locations, our farms use Crop Rotation, Organic Farming, and Intercropping practices to ensure sustainable and high-quality produce.
